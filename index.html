Process Flow

Step 01
Preparation

Clean dust. Level surface. Wear Nitrile gloves. Ensure temperature is 24-26°C.

Step 02
Weighing

Precision weigh 3:1 ratio by weight (not volume). Use a digital scale.

Step 03
Mixing

Mix slowly for 3-5 minutes. Scrape sides and bottom. Transfer to a second cup.

Step 04
Pour & Cure

Pour. Torch bubbles after 10 mins. Cover. Demold in 24 hrs. Full cure in 3 days.

Technical FAQ

Is a pressure pot required?

No. Our low viscosity (180 mPas) allows bubbles to rise and pop naturally. A simple torch pass is enough.

What is the max pour depth?

For Transparent Plus, we recommend 20-25mm per layer. For deeper pours, use our Deep Cast series.

Is it food safe?

Yes, once fully cured (7 days), it is inert and safe for incidental food contact (like trays).
