Technical FAQ

Is a pressure pot required?

No. Our low viscosity (180 mPas) allows bubbles to rise and pop naturally.

What is the max pour depth?

For Transparent Plus, we recommend 20-25mm per layer. For deeper pours, use our Deep Cast series.

Is it food safe?

Yes, once fully cured (7 days), it is inert and safe for incidental food contact.
