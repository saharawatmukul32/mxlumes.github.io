The Pour Protocol

Step 01

Measure by Weight

Do not measure by volume. Place cup on scale. Pour 3 parts Resin, then 1 part Hardener.

Step 02

Mix & Transfer

Mix for 3 minutes. Scrape sides. Pour into a second cup and mix again (Double Cup Method).

Step 03

Pour & Cure

Pour into mold. Wait 5 mins for bubbles to rise. Torch surface. Cover to protect from dust.

✓ The Dos

  • Wear nitrile gloves and respirator.
  • Work in a temperature controlled room (22-25°C).
  • Seal wood/porous objects before pouring.

✕ The Don'ts

  • Do not mix by volume (cups).
  • Do not scrape the cup while pouring into mold.
  • Do not introduce water/moisture to resin.
⚠️

Chemical Hazard Warning

Epoxy resin can cause skin irritation and sensitization. Avoid breathing fumes. In case of eye contact, rinse immediately and seek medical advice. Keep out of reach of children.
